PRIVACY POLICY // MATHHUB.PRO

At MathHub Pro, accessible from https://mathhub.pro, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by MathHub Pro and how we use it.

Log Files & Cookies

MathHub Pro follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. We use cookies to store information including visitors' preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to mathhub.pro and other sites on the internet.

Firebase & Data Security

For users who utilize our Login System, your data is encrypted via Firebase. We do not share your email with third-party marketers.
