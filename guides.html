1. FINANCIAL MATHEMATICS & TAXATION

1.1 The GST Protocol (Goods and Services Tax)

In the 2026 Indian economic environment, the GST Calculator is our most utilized tool. It allows businesses in Mancherial and across the nation to calculate tax breakdowns across the 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28% slabs. The mathematical logic follows the standard derivation:

$$Total = Net Price + \left( \frac{Net Price \times GST Rate}{100} \right)$$

For reverse calculations to find the base price from a GST-inclusive total:

$$Base Price = \frac{Inclusive Total}{1 + \frac{GST Rate}{100}}$$

1.2 Interest & Loan EMI Dynamics

Our Compound Interest and Loan EMI tools utilize time-value-of-money formulas to predict long-term financial health. For any principal amount $P$, at an annual rate $r$, over $t$ years with $n$ compounding periods:

$$A = P \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}$$
